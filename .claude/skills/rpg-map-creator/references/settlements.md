## Settlement Sizes & Types

### Hamlet
**Population:** 20-100 people
**Visual Characteristics:** Cluster of simple buildings, dirt paths, communal well, small fields
**Layout:** Informal arrangement around crossroads or water source
**Color Palette:** Natural wood browns, thatched roofs (yellows/greys), earth tones
**Atmosphere:** Rustic, peaceful, agrarian
**Key Features:** 3-10 buildings, small shrine, communal spaces, surrounding farmland

### Village
**Population:** 100-1,000 people
**Visual Characteristics:** Organized clusters of homes, central square, small market area, place of worship
**Layout:** Main road with buildings on either side, radiating paths
**Color Palette:** Mixed materials (wood, stone), varied roof colors, gardens
**Atmosphere:** Community-focused, modest prosperity, close-knit
**Key Features:** Inn/tavern, general store, blacksmith, mill, 20-50 buildings, village green

### Town
**Population:** 1,000-10,000 people
**Visual Characteristics:** Defined streets, multi-story buildings, market square, walls or palisades (sometimes)
**Layout:** Grid or organic street pattern, distinct districts emerging
**Color Palette:** More refined materials (stone, brick), painted signs, varied roofs
**Atmosphere:** Bustling, commercial, organized
**Key Features:** Multiple shops, guildhalls, town hall, guards, 100+ buildings, marketplace

### City
**Population:** 10,000-100,000+ people
**Visual Characteristics:** Dense urban core, multiple districts, impressive architecture, walls/fortifications
**Layout:** Complex street networks, grand boulevards, organized districts
**Color Palette:** Stone/brick dominance, colorful banners, varied materials showing wealth disparity
**Atmosphere:** Diverse, crowded, centers of power and culture
**Key Features:** Temples, palaces, markets, slums, universities, thousands of buildings, monuments

### Metropolis
**Population:** 100,000+ people
**Visual Characteristics:** Massive urban sprawl, towering structures, distinct quarters, architectural marvels
**Layout:** Highly organized or chaotic depending on age, multiple layers (sometimes), major roadways
**Color Palette:** Grand and varied, showing extreme wealth and poverty side-by-side
**Atmosphere:** Overwhelming, center of civilization/power, never sleeps
**Key Features:** Great temples, royal palaces, extensive walls, major ports/trade, diverse populations

## Fantasy Architectural Styles

### Medieval European
**Visual Characteristics:** Stone and timber construction, steep roofs, narrow windows, defensive features
**Materials:** Timber frames, wattle and daub, stone (wealth dependent), thatch or slate roofs
**Color Palette:** Grey/brown stone, dark wood beams, white/cream plaster, dark roofs
**Distinctive Elements:** Half-timbering, castle keeps, Gothic arches, cobblestone streets
**Atmosphere:** Classic fantasy, familiar, structured
**Examples:** Castles, cathedrals, guild halls, fortified manors

### Elven
**Visual Characteristics:** Organic integration with nature, flowing lines, graceful arches, living wood construction
**Materials:** Living trees shaped as buildings, white stone, crystal, silver/gold accents
**Color Palette:** Natural greens, whites, silvers, golds, soft pastels
**Distinctive Elements:** Spiral architecture, platforms in trees, no straight lines, seamless nature fusion
**Atmosphere:** Ethereal, timeless, harmonious with environment
**Examples:** Tree cities, crystal spires, garden palaces, forest halls

### Dwarven
**Visual Characteristics:** Carved from solid stone, geometric precision, underground construction, heavy fortification
**Materials:** Granite, marble, iron, bronze, precious metals
**Color Palette:** Stone greys, browns, metallic golds/bronzes, torch/lava glow
**Distinctive Elements:** Massive pillars, vaulted ceilings, geometric patterns, runes, metalwork
**Atmosphere:** Sturdy, ancient, underground grandeur, industrious
**Examples:** Mountain halls, underground cities, forges, treasure vaults

### Desert/Arabian
**Visual Characteristics:** Adobe/sandstone construction, domes and minarets, inner courtyards, decorative tilework
**Materials:** Sandstone, mud brick, ceramic tiles, fabric awnings
**Color Palette:** Sandy yellows/browns, white, turquoise tiles, bright fabric colors
**Distinctive Elements:** Onion domes, pointed arches, intricate geometric patterns, fountains
**Atmosphere:** Hot, exotic, ornate, oasis of civilization
**Examples:** Palaces, bazaars, caravanserais, mosques

### Asian-Inspired
**Visual Characteristics:** Multi-tiered curved roofs, wooden construction, sliding panels, ornate detailing
**Materials:** Wood (lacquered), paper screens, ceramic tiles, stone foundations
**Color Palette:** Red lacquer, gold trim, dark wood, white walls, green/blue tiles
**Distinctive Elements:** Upturned eaves, pagoda towers, gardens, ceremonial gates
**Atmosphere:** Harmonious, refined, spiritual
**Examples:** Temples, pagodas, imperial palaces, gardens

### Orcish/Tribal
**Visual Characteristics:** Crude but effective construction, animal motifs, trophies, intimidating design
**Materials:** Rough-hewn wood, bones, hide, scavenged metal, stone
**Color Palette:** Dark browns, greys, reds (blood/war paint), bone whites
**Distinctive Elements:** Skulls as decoration, spikes, tribal symbols, war trophies
**Atmosphere:** Brutal, intimidating, primitive strength
**Examples:** Longhouses, war camps, tribal totems, crude fortifications

### Nomadic/Tent Cities
**Visual Characteristics:** Temporary structures, fabric construction, portable, clustered arrangements
**Materials:** Canvas/fabric, wooden poles, animal hides, ropes
**Color Palette:** Varied bright fabrics, natural materials
**Distinctive Elements:** Large tent pavilions, guy lines, portable furniture, wind-blown banners
**Atmosphere:** Temporary, mobile, community-oriented
**Examples:** Yurts, pavilions, caravan encampments, festival grounds

## Sci-Fi Architectural Styles

### Cyberpunk Urban
**Visual Characteristics:** Towering megastructures, neon signs, dense vertical construction, gritty lower levels
**Materials:** Concrete, steel, glass, holographic displays, exposed wiring
**Color Palette:** Neon pinks/blues/greens, dark greys, rust, rain-slicked reflections
**Distinctive Elements:** Neon advertisements, elevated highways, corporate towers, slums below
**Atmosphere:** Dystopian, overcrowded, high-tech low-life, perpetual night/rain
**Examples:** Corporate arcologies, street markets, underground clubs

### Sleek Futuristic
**Visual Characteristics:** Clean lines, smooth surfaces, minimalist design, integrated technology
**Materials:** Advanced polymers, smart glass, energy fields, holographic elements
**Color Palette:** Whites, silvers, soft blues, glowing accents
**Distinctive Elements:** Floating elements, seamless surfaces, holographic interfaces, perfect symmetry
**Atmosphere:** Utopian, advanced, sterile, optimistic future
**Examples:** Space stations, research facilities, utopian cities

### Post-Apocalyptic
**Visual Characteristics:** Ruined pre-war structures, makeshift repairs, scavenged materials, defensive modifications
**Materials:** Salvaged metal, scrap, jury-rigged tech, reinforced ruins
**Color Palette:** Rust browns, greys, faded colors, dust, ash
**Distinctive Elements:** Patchwork repairs, barricades, warning signs, radiation symbols
**Atmosphere:** Desperate survival, decay, dangerous, resource-scarce
**Examples:** Fortified ruins, survivor camps, raider outposts

### Space Colony
**Visual Characteristics:** Modular construction, domed or enclosed habitats, efficient use of space, life support visible
**Materials:** Metal alloys, transparent domes, solar panels, radiation shielding
**Color Palette:** Industrial greys, warning yellows, functional markings
**Distinctive Elements:** Airlocks, hydroponics, solar arrays, docking bays, observation windows
**Atmosphere:** Frontier, isolated, humanity's reach, fragile existence
**Examples:** Moon bases, Mars colonies, asteroid habitats, space stations

### Alien/Exotic
**Visual Characteristics:** Non-Euclidean geometry, organic or crystalline structures, impossible physics
**Materials:** Unknown/alien materials, living architecture, energy constructs
**Color Palette:** Unusual color combinations, bioluminescence, shifting patterns
**Distinctive Elements:** Defying gravity, biological integration, purpose unknown to humans
**Atmosphere:** Alien, incomprehensible, wondrous or terrifying
**Examples:** Ancient alien ruins, living ships, dimensional structures

## Defensive Structures

### Palisade
**Visual Characteristics:** Wooden wall of vertical logs, walkway on top, watchtowers at intervals
**Materials:** Logs, sharpened stakes, wooden platforms
**Color Palette:** Brown wood, earth
**Defensive Features:** Height barrier, archer platforms, main gate
**Scale:** Surrounds villages or small towns

### Stone Walls
**Visual Characteristics:** Thick stone construction, crenellations, arrow slits, gatehouse
**Materials:** Cut stone, mortar, iron-banded gates
**Color Palette:** Grey/brown stone
**Defensive Features:** Withstands siege, multiple murder holes, oil drops
**Scale:** Surrounds towns and cities

### Castle/Fortress
**Visual Characteristics:** Multiple walls (curtain walls), keep/donjon, towers, moat, barbican
**Materials:** Stone masonry, iron reinforcements
**Color Palette:** Grey stone, banners in house colors
**Defensive Features:** Concentric defenses, killing fields, strategic position
**Scale:** Dominates landscape, controls territory

### Magical Barriers
**Visual Characteristics:** Shimmering energy fields, floating runes, translucent domes
**Materials:** Pure magical energy, spell anchors, focusing crystals
**Color Palette:** Glowing blues, purples, golds, ethereal light
**Defensive Features:** Impenetrable to specified threats, can be selective
**Scale:** Any size, from single building to entire city

### Fortified Walls (Modern/Sci-Fi)
**Visual Characteristics:** Reinforced concrete/metal, automated defenses, sensor arrays, energy shields
**Materials:** Advanced composites, force field generators, weapon emplacements
**Color Palette:** Industrial greys, warning stripes, status lights
**Defensive Features:** Automated turrets, shields, surveillance, kill zones
**Scale:** Perimeter security for facilities or settlements

## Special Locations

### Temples/Churches
**Visual Characteristics:** Tall spires or domes, religious iconography, grand entrances, bells/calls to prayer
**Layout:** Central nave, altar, side chapels, courtyard
**Atmosphere:** Sacred, peaceful or imposing, architecturally impressive
**Visual Elements:** Stained glass, religious symbols, statuary, meditation gardens

### Markets/Bazaars
**Visual Characteristics:** Colorful stalls, awnings, crowds, goods displayed, chaotic organization
**Layout:** Central square, covered alleys, or street market
**Atmosphere:** Bustling, noisy, colorful, sensory overload
**Visual Elements:** Hanging goods, bright fabrics, food stalls, merchant signs

### Ports/Harbors
**Visual Characteristics:** Docks, ships, warehouses, cranes, fishy/salty atmosphere
**Layout:** Waterfront with piers, storage areas, maritime facilities
**Atmosphere:** Busy, cosmopolitan, rough, gateway to world
**Visual Elements:** Ships (size/type varies), cargo, seagulls, maritime equipment

### Academy/University
**Visual Characteristics:** Multiple grand buildings, libraries, dormitories, gardens, towers (for astronomy/magic)
**Layout:** Campus with courtyards, cloisters, lecture halls
**Atmosphere:** Scholarly, quiet, intellectual hub
**Visual Elements:** Books visible through windows, students, research equipment, ancient architecture

### Ruins
**Visual Characteristics:** Crumbling walls, overgrown vegetation, partially collapsed structures, weathering
**Layout:** Ghost of original layout, dangerous areas
**Atmosphere:** Abandoned, melancholic, mysterious, dangerous
**Visual Elements:** Broken columns, moss/vines, partially intact structures, hints of former glory

### Wizard Tower
**Visual Characteristics:** Tall isolated tower, magical effects, strange architecture, observatory dome
**Layout:** Vertical design, workshop levels, library, living quarters, sanctum at top
**Atmosphere:** Mysterious, powerful, isolated, arcane
**Visual Elements:** Glowing windows, floating objects, magical barriers, strange lights

### Tavern/Inn
**Visual Characteristics:** Welcoming entrance, hanging sign, multiple stories, stables
**Layout:** Common room, bar, kitchen, rooms upstairs, stable
**Atmosphere:** Warm, social hub, safe haven
**Visual Elements:** Wooden construction, fireplace light, hanging sign (often with image)

## Urban Features

### Streets & Roads
**Types:** Dirt paths, cobblestone, paved, elevated highways
**Visual Elements:** Worn paths, drainage, street lamps, signage
**Atmosphere:** Varies from muddy village path to grand boulevard

### Bridges
**Types:** Stone arch, wooden, suspension, magical (floating), technological (energy)
**Visual Elements:** Architectural style matching culture, defensive towers (sometimes), tolls
**Atmosphere:** Connection, strategic importance, often picturesque

### City Walls & Gates
**Visual Elements:** Imposing structure, guard posts, queues for entry, portcullis
**Atmosphere:** Security, checkpoint, controlled access
**Defensive:** Murder holes, gate mechanisms, wall towers

### Districts/Quarters
**Wealthy Quarter:** Fine stone buildings, gardens, clean streets, guards
**Merchant District:** Shops, signs, medium quality, busy
**Poor Quarter/Slums:** Ramshackle buildings, narrow alleys, overcrowded, unsafe
**Craftsman Quarter:** Workshops, guild halls, apprentice housing, smoke from forges
**Port District:** Warehouses, taverns, rough atmosphere, maritime goods

### Parks & Gardens
**Visual Elements:** Trees, fountains, statuary, paths, benches
**Atmosphere:** Peaceful, maintained, public space
**Purpose:** Recreation, social gathering, beauty

### Monuments & Statues
**Types:** Statues of heroes, memorial columns, arches, fountains
**Visual Elements:** Grand scale, often in squares, inscriptions
**Atmosphere:** Civic pride, historical importance, gathering landmark

### Underground/Sewers
**Visual Elements:** Brick tunnels, flowing water (often filthy), grates above, torchlight
**Atmosphere:** Dark, dangerous, secret passages, home to outcasts
**Purpose:** Waste disposal, secret travel, hideouts
