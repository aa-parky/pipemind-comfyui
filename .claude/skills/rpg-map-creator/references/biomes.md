## Forest Biomes

### Temperate Deciduous Forest
**Visual Characteristics:** Broad-leafed trees in seasonal colors, dappled sunlight through canopy, mossy ground cover, fallen logs, clearings with wildflowers
**Color Palette:** Greens (spring/summer), oranges/reds/yellows (autumn), browns (winter)
**Atmosphere:** Peaceful, filtered light, gentle shadows
**Common Features:** Game trails, streams, woodland clearings, ancient oaks

### Temperate Coniferous Forest
**Visual Characteristics:** Tall evergreen trees (pine, fir, spruce), needle-covered ground, sparse undergrowth, straight trunks reaching skyward
**Color Palette:** Deep greens, browns, grey-green moss
**Atmosphere:** Quiet, dim under canopy, misty mornings
**Common Features:** Pine needle carpet, fallen cones, rocky outcrops

### Tropical Rainforest
**Visual Characteristics:** Dense multi-layered canopy, hanging vines, massive buttress roots, vibrant flowers, thick undergrowth
**Color Palette:** Rich greens, vibrant flower colors (reds, purples, yellows), dark shadows
**Atmosphere:** Humid, steamy, filtered emerald light, sounds of wildlife
**Common Features:** Massive ancient trees, vine bridges, waterfalls, exotic plants

### Boreal Forest (Taiga)
**Visual Characteristics:** Spindly conifers, snow-laden branches in winter, sparse vegetation, lichen-covered rocks
**Color Palette:** Dark greens, whites (snow), greys, pale blues
**Atmosphere:** Cold, stark, quiet, long shadows
**Common Features:** Frozen streams, ice-covered lakes, animal tracks in snow

### Dark/Haunted Forest
**Visual Characteristics:** Gnarled twisted trees, thick fog, minimal undergrowth, dead branches, ominous shadows
**Color Palette:** Dark greens, blacks, greys, sickly yellows, purple shadows
**Atmosphere:** Foreboding, mysterious, oppressive darkness, unnatural silence
**Common Features:** Ancient trees with faces, will-o-wisps, abandoned structures, thorny vines

## Grassland & Plains

### Temperate Grassland
**Visual Characteristics:** Rolling hills of tall grass, wildflowers scattered throughout, wide open skies, gentle slopes
**Color Palette:** Golden yellows, greens, blue skies, white clouds
**Atmosphere:** Open, windswept, peaceful, vast
**Common Features:** Lone trees, streams cutting through valleys, rocks jutting from ground

### Prairie
**Visual Characteristics:** Sea of grasses swaying in wind, occasional copses of trees, distant horizons
**Color Palette:** Amber, gold, green (depending on season), big sky blues
**Atmosphere:** Freedom, isolation, endless expanse
**Common Features:** Buffalo wallows, prairie dog towns, wildflower patches

### Savanna
**Visual Characteristics:** Scattered acacia trees, tall golden grass, flat-topped thorny trees, red earth showing through
**Color Palette:** Golds, browns, dusty reds, bright blue sky
**Atmosphere:** Hot, dry, wide open, dramatic skies
**Common Features:** Termite mounds, water holes, baobab trees, scattered rocks

### Steppe
**Visual Characteristics:** Semi-arid grassland, short grasses, sparse vegetation, rocky patches
**Color Palette:** Dusty browns, pale greens, greys
**Atmosphere:** Harsh, windswept, barren beauty
**Common Features:** Stone circles, dry riverbeds, hardy shrubs

## Desert Biomes

### Hot Sandy Desert
**Visual Characteristics:** Rolling sand dunes, rippled patterns from wind, sparse vegetation, mirages in distance
**Color Palette:** Golds, oranges, yellows, bright blues (sky), harsh whites (sun)
**Atmosphere:** Scorching heat, shimmering air, stark beauty, isolation
**Common Features:** Oasis with palm trees, rock formations, bleached bones, ancient ruins half-buried

### Rocky Desert (Badlands)
**Visual Characteristics:** Layered sedimentary rock formations, deep canyons, mesas, colorful strata
**Color Palette:** Reds, oranges, purples, browns, ochres
**Atmosphere:** Harsh, dramatic, alien beauty
**Common Features:** Natural arches, slot canyons, hoodoos, dry washes

### Cold Desert
**Visual Characteristics:** Rocky plains with sparse vegetation, cold winds, snow in winter, gravel and stone
**Color Palette:** Greys, browns, whites (snow), pale blues
**Atmosphere:** Desolate, cold, windswept
**Common Features:** Rock fields, ice patches, hardy shrubs, dust storms

### Oasis
**Visual Characteristics:** Lush greenery around water source, palm trees, reeds, sharp contrast with surrounding desert
**Color Palette:** Vibrant greens, blues (water), contrasting with desert yellows/browns
**Atmosphere:** Relief, life-giving, sanctuary
**Common Features:** Date palms, clear pools, flowering plants, traders' camps

## Mountain & Highland

### Alpine Mountains
**Visual Characteristics:** Snow-capped peaks, steep rocky faces, glaciers, alpine meadows at lower elevations
**Color Palette:** White (snow), grey (rock), blue (ice/sky), bright wildflower colors in meadows
**Atmosphere:** Majestic, cold, thin air, dramatic
**Common Features:** Glacial lakes, moraines, mountain passes, avalanche paths

### Volcanic Mountains
**Visual Characteristics:** Cone-shaped peaks, black/red volcanic rock, steam vents, lava flows (active), obsidian deposits
**Color Palette:** Blacks, dark greys, reds (lava), orange glows, grey ash
**Atmosphere:** Dangerous, primordial, powerful
**Common Features:** Calderas, lava tubes, hot springs, ash fields

### Highland Moors
**Visual Characteristics:** Rolling hills covered in heather, rocky outcrops, boggy areas, windswept vegetation
**Color Palette:** Purple (heather), browns, dark greens, grey stones
**Atmosphere:** Bleak, windswept, melancholic beauty
**Common Features:** Standing stones, ruins, peat bogs, sheep paths

### Mountain Valleys
**Visual Characteristics:** Steep sides rising to peaks, river or stream through center, meadows or forests on valley floor
**Color Palette:** Greens (vegetation), greys/whites (peaks), blue (water)
**Atmosphere:** Sheltered, peaceful, dramatic backdrop
**Common Features:** Waterfalls cascading from heights, villages nestled in valley, mountain paths

## Arctic & Tundra

### Arctic Tundra
**Visual Characteristics:** Flat or gently rolling landscape, permafrost, no trees, low vegetation (moss, lichen, small shrubs)
**Color Palette:** Whites, pale blues, greys, browns, brief summer wildflower colors
**Atmosphere:** Harsh, barren, endless, brutal cold
**Common Features:** Ice formations, frozen lakes, aurora borealis, animal tracks

### Ice Shelf/Glacier
**Visual Characteristics:** Massive walls of blue-white ice, crevasses, icebergs breaking off, crystalline formations
**Color Palette:** Blues (ice), whites, pale cyans, grey (rocks)
**Atmosphere:** Pristine, dangerous, otherworldly
**Common Features:** Ice caves, pressure ridges, meltwater streams, ice floes

## Aquatic Environments

### Coastal/Beach
**Visual Characteristics:** Sandy or rocky shore, waves breaking, dunes, tidepools, driftwood
**Color Palette:** Blues/greens (water), yellows/whites (sand), greys (rocks)
**Atmosphere:** Dynamic, rhythmic waves, salt air, open
**Common Features:** Shells, seaweed, cliffs, lighthouses, fishing villages

### Ocean/Sea
**Visual Characteristics:** Endless water, waves, whitecaps, changing colors with depth, distant horizons
**Color Palette:** Deep blues, greens, greys (storms), whites (foam)
**Atmosphere:** Vast, powerful, mysterious depths
**Common Features:** Islands in distance, sea stacks, marine life, ships

### River
**Visual Characteristics:** Flowing water, rapids or calm stretches, riverbanks with vegetation, meanders
**Color Palette:** Blues/greens (water), browns (muddy banks), greens (vegetation)
**Atmosphere:** Life-giving, dynamic, travel route
**Common Features:** Waterfalls, fords, bridges, riverside settlements, fishing spots

### Lake
**Visual Characteristics:** Still water reflecting sky, shoreline, can be clear or murky, surrounded by terrain
**Color Palette:** Blues, greens, grey (overcast), mirror reflections
**Atmosphere:** Peaceful, mysterious depths, gathering place
**Common Features:** Docks, islands, reeds, fishing boats, lakeside villages

### Underground Lake/River
**Visual Characteristics:** Dark water, phosphorescent fungi providing eerie light, stalactites reflecting in water
**Color Palette:** Deep blacks, blues, eerie greens/blues (bioluminescence)
**Atmosphere:** Mysterious, echoing, otherworldly
**Common Features:** Stone pillars, cave formations, underground waterfalls, blind fish

## Wetlands

### Swamp
**Visual Characteristics:** Standing water, cypress or mangrove trees, hanging moss, murky water, thick vegetation
**Color Palette:** Dark greens, browns, blacks (water), grey-green moss
**Atmosphere:** Humid, mysterious, dangerous, teeming with hidden life
**Common Features:** Fallen logs, lily pads, twisted roots, mist, ruins sinking into water

### Marsh
**Visual Characteristics:** Grasses and reeds in shallow water, winding channels, mudflats
**Color Palette:** Greens, browns, blues (water/sky)
**Atmosphere:** Open, alive with birds, squelchy
**Common Features:** Reed beds, winding waterways, water birds, fish

### Bog
**Visual Characteristics:** Spongy peat moss ground, carnivorous plants, stagnant pools, stunted trees
**Color Palette:** Browns, dark greens, blacks, rust reds
**Atmosphere:** Desolate, treacherous ground, primordial
**Common Features:** Will-o-wisps, peat deposits, preserved remains, rare plants

### Mangrove
**Visual Characteristics:** Trees on stilted roots in brackish water, dense tangles, coastal location
**Color Palette:** Greens, browns, blues, greys
**Atmosphere:** Dense, maze-like, teeming with life
**Common Features:** Tidal zones, intricate root systems, coastal creatures

## Volcanic Regions

### Active Volcano
**Visual Characteristics:** Smoking crater, lava flows glowing orange-red, ash clouds, scorched earth
**Color Palette:** Blacks, greys (ash), reds/oranges (lava), dark smoke
**Atmosphere:** Dangerous, powerful, primal force
**Common Features:** Lava lakes, fumaroles, volcanic bombs, rivers of lava

### Volcanic Wasteland
**Visual Characteristics:** Hardened lava fields, obsidian deposits, steam vents, barren black rock
**Color Palette:** Blacks, dark greys, rust reds, ash grey
**Atmosphere:** Desolate, harsh, recently devastated
**Common Features:** Lava tubes, geysers, hot springs, pioneer plants

### Geothermal Area
**Visual Characteristics:** Colorful mineral deposits, hot springs, geysers, bubbling mud pots, steam
**Color Palette:** Yellows, oranges, reds (sulfur and minerals), blues/greens (pools)
**Atmosphere:** Otherworldly, steaming, colorful, dangerous
**Common Features:** Terraced pools, geyser eruptions, fumaroles, crystalline deposits

## Magical & Exotic Biomes

### Crystal Forest
**Visual Characteristics:** Trees or formations made of crystal, prismatic light effects, geometric shapes, glowing elements
**Color Palette:** Translucent colors, rainbow refractions, glowing hues
**Atmosphere:** Ethereal, magical, surreal
**Common Features:** Singing crystals, light beams, floating particles, harmonic resonance

### Fungal Forest
**Visual Characteristics:** Giant mushrooms as trees, bioluminescent fungi, spore clouds, mycelium networks
**Color Palette:** Deep purples, blues, greens (bioluminescence), browns, whites
**Atmosphere:** Alien, mysterious, glowing, surreal
**Common Features:** Mushroom caps as platforms, glowing spores, fungal creatures

### Floating Islands
**Visual Characteristics:** Land masses floating in sky/void, waterfalls cascading off edges, vegetation on top
**Color Palette:** Sky blues, clouds, earthy colors on islands, magical energies
**Atmosphere:** Impossible, wondrous, gravity-defying
**Common Features:** Sky bridges, flying creatures, ancient ruins, magical crystals

### Corrupted/Blighted Land
**Visual Characteristics:** Twisted vegetation, sickly colors, dead/dying plant life, unnatural growths, toxic pools
**Color Palette:** Sickly greens, purples, blacks, greys, rust reds
**Atmosphere:** Wrong, corrupted, dangerous, oppressive
**Common Features:** Mutated creatures, spreading corruption, dark magic residue

### Fey Wilds
**Visual Characteristics:** Impossibly vibrant colors, oversized flora, glowing plants, surreal physics, dreamlike
**Color Palette:** Hyper-saturated colors, glows, sparkles, shifting hues
**Atmosphere:** Enchanted, dangerous beauty, timeless, otherworldly
**Common Features:** Fairy rings, toadstool circles, magic pools, impossible architecture

## Underground Environments

### Cave System
**Visual Characteristics:** Stone passages, stalactites and stalagmites, underground pools, varying widths
**Color Palette:** Greys, browns, blacks, mineral colors (whites, ambers)
**Atmosphere:** Dark, echo-filled, claustrophobic or vast
**Common Features:** Columns, flowstone, cave pearls, underground streams

### Underdark/Deep Caverns
**Visual Characteristics:** Massive cavern spaces, bioluminescent fungi/crystals, alien stone formations, chasms
**Color Palette:** Blacks, deep purples, blues, eerie glows
**Atmosphere:** Alien, dangerous, vast underground world
**Common Features:** Underground cities, strange fungi forests, abyssal chasms, crystal formations

### Mine (Abandoned/Active)
**Visual Characteristics:** Carved tunnels, support timbers, ore veins, mine carts, tools
**Color Palette:** Browns (wood), greys (stone), metallic colors (ore), torch light
**Atmosphere:** Industrial, claustrophobic, echoing
**Common Features:** Shafts, ore deposits, collapsed sections, mining equipment

### Dungeon/Tomb
**Visual Characteristics:** Worked stone, architectural elements, carved reliefs, crypts, passages
**Color Palette:** Stone greys, dusty browns, torch light, darkness
**Atmosphere:** Ancient, foreboding, trapped, haunted
**Common Features:** Sarcophagi, treasure chambers, traps, ancient inscriptions
