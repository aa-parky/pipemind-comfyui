## High Fantasy

**Core Concept:** Classic heroic fantasy with clear good vs evil, magic is wondrous, epic quests

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Bright, vibrant colors
- Majestic castles and towers
- Ancient forests with mystical quality
- Clear skies or dramatic clouds
- Magic appears as bright, colorful energy
- Noble knights and wise wizards

**Color Palette:** Rich jewel tones, golds, silvers, pure whites, deep forest greens, sky blues

**Atmosphere:** Epic, heroic, wondrous, optimistic despite danger

**Common Elements:**
- Shining castles
- Elven tree cities
- Dwarven mountain halls
- Ancient magical artifacts glowing with power
- Dragons (majestic and terrible)
- Clear distinctions between good/evil forces
- Pastoral villages
- Sacred temples with divine light

**Map Features:**
- Well-defined kingdoms with borders
- Ancient ruins with obvious magical purpose
- Sacred groves and holy sites
- Roads connecting civilization
- Wilderness as adventure space

**Lighting:** Dramatic but hopeful - golden sunrises, star-filled nights, magical glows

**Example Prompt Elements:** "epic fantasy landscape, majestic castle on hill, vibrant colors, heroic atmosphere, golden light, detailed medieval architecture, fantasy art style"

## Dark Fantasy

**Core Concept:** Fantasy with horror elements, grimdark tone, magic has a cost, morally grey

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Muted, desaturated colors
- Gothic architecture
- Ominous skies
- Shadows and fog
- Magic appears corrupted or dangerous
- Decaying grandeur

**Color Palette:** Greys, dark blues, sickly greens, blood reds, faded browns, tarnished metals

**Atmosphere:** Oppressive, dangerous, Gothic horror, no easy victories

**Common Elements:**
- Crumbling castles
- Haunted forests
- Cursed lands
- Undead creatures
- Dark sorcery with visible corruption
- Plague-ridden villages
- Ominous monoliths
- Blood magic circles

**Map Features:**
- Blighted regions spreading corruption
- Ruins more common than thriving settlements
- Dangerous wilderness
- Cursed locations marked with warning signs
- Underground catacombs

**Lighting:** Low, dramatic contrast - dim torchlight, sickly moon, unnatural glows, deep shadows

**Example Prompt Elements:** "dark fantasy landscape, Gothic ruins, ominous atmosphere, muted colors, fog, dramatic shadows, corrupted magic, grimdark aesthetic"

## Sword & Sorcery

**Core Concept:** Pulp adventure, individual heroes, dangerous magic, ancient civilizations, visceral action

**Visual Characteristics:**
- High contrast
- Exotic locations
- Ancient ruins
- Dangerous, untamed wilderness
- Magic is powerful but risky
- Barbarian aesthetic mixed with decadent civilizations

**Color Palette:** Bold primaries, bronze and copper, sandstone, blood red, jungle greens

**Atmosphere:** Adventure, danger, exotic, primal

**Common Elements:**
- Jungle-covered ruins
- Desert kingdoms
- Barbaric tribes
- Decadent city-states
- Ancient sorcery
- Monster-haunted wilderness
- Treasure hordes
- Serpent cults

**Map Features:**
- Isolated settlements in hostile wilderness
- Ancient ruins everywhere
- Uncharted territories
- Lost cities
- Natural barriers (jungles, deserts, mountains)

**Lighting:** Dramatic - blazing sun, torch-lit dungeons, mysterious shadows, gleaming treasure

**Example Prompt Elements:** "sword and sorcery scene, ancient ruins in jungle, bronze age aesthetic, dramatic lighting, exotic location, adventure art style, pulp magazine cover"

## Steampunk

**Core Concept:** Victorian era + steam technology, brass and copper, industrial revolution aesthetics

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Brass, copper, bronze everywhere
- Exposed gears and mechanisms
- Steam and smoke
- Victorian architecture with industrial elements
- Airships and mechanical contraptions
- Clockwork precision

**Color Palette:** Brass golds, coppers, industrial browns, smoke greys, Victorian burgundy, steel

**Atmosphere:** Industrial revolution, innovation, class divide, smog-filled skies

**Common Elements:**
- Clock towers
- Airship docks
- Steam factories
- Gear-covered buildings
- Pneumatic tubes
- Automaton workers
- Victorian mansions with mechanical additions
- Railway stations

**Map Features:**
- Industrial districts with factories
- Airship routes and docking towers
- Railway networks
- Underground steam tunnels
- Smog-covered lower districts
- Upper class elevated areas

**Lighting:** Gas lamps, steam diffusion, industrial orange glows, brass reflections

**Example Prompt Elements:** "steampunk cityscape, Victorian architecture, brass and copper machinery, steam-powered airships, industrial aesthetic, gears and clockwork, gaslight, detailed mechanical elements"

## Dieselpunk

**Core Concept:** 1920s-1950s aesthetics + diesel technology, Art Deco, retrofuturistic, often wartime

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Art Deco architecture
- Chunky, riveted metal
- Diesel engines and exhaust
- Military aesthetics
- Chrome and steel
- Propaganda posters

**Color Palette:** Olive drab, military greys, rust, chrome silver, Art Deco golds, patriotic reds/blues

**Atmosphere:** Wartime, noir, gritty optimism, diesel fumes, jazz age

**Common Elements:**
- Art Deco skyscrapers
- Zeppelins and diesel-punk aircraft
- Military vehicles
- Radio towers
- Underground resistance bases
- Mechanized warfare
- Propaganda
- Jazz clubs and speakeasies

**Map Features:**
- Military installations
- Strategic locations
- War-torn landscapes
- Underground bunkers
- Supply routes
- Fortified cities
- No-man's land

**Lighting:** Harsh spotlights, searchlights, film noir shadows, neon signs, diesel fires

**Example Prompt Elements:** "dieselpunk landscape, Art Deco architecture, military aesthetic, riveted metal, 1940s retrofuturistic, diesel-powered vehicles, noir atmosphere, dramatic lighting"

## Cyberpunk

**Core Concept:** High tech, low life, corporate dystopia, cybernetic enhancement, digital worlds

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Neon everywhere
- Towering megastructures
- Holographic advertisements
- Rain-slicked streets
- Cybernetic implants
- Dense vertical cities
- Digital interfaces

**Color Palette:** Neon pink/blue/green, dark greys/blacks, chrome, holographic colors, rain reflections

**Atmosphere:** Dystopian, noir, high-tech but decaying, corporate control, perpetual night

**Common Elements:**
- Megacorporation towers
- Street-level markets
- Cyberspace representations
- Body modification clinics
- Hacker dens
- Underground clubs
- Automated security
- Holographic billboards

**Map Features:**
- Vertical stratification (rich above, poor below)
- Corporate zones
- No-go zones
- Underground networks
- Digital/cyberspace overlay
- Sprawling metropolis
- Automated districts

**Lighting:** Neon dominance, holographic glows, harsh fluorescent, deep shadows, rain reflections

**Example Prompt Elements:** "cyberpunk cityscape, neon lights, rain-slicked streets, holographic advertisements, towering megastructures, night scene, dystopian atmosphere, high-tech aesthetic, dramatic neon lighting"

## Space Opera

**Core Concept:** Epic space adventure, galactic civilizations, faster-than-light travel, cosmic scale

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Massive space structures
- Sleek starships
- Alien architectures
- Planetary diversity
- Energy weapons and shields
- Advanced technology as commonplace

**Color Palette:** Cosmic blues/purples, clean whites/silvers, alien color schemes, energy glows, starfield blacks

**Atmosphere:** Epic scope, sense of wonder, diverse civilizations, frontier spirit

**Common Elements:**
- Space stations
- Alien cities
- Starship interiors
- Exotic planets
- Trade hubs
- Military installations
- Ancient alien ruins
- Asteroid bases

**Map Features:**
- Star systems
- Trade routes through space
- Inhabited planets
- Space anomalies
- Empire territories
- Neutral zones
- Unexplored sectors

**Lighting:** Stars, nebulae, artificial lighting, bioluminescence (alien), energy glows, cockpit lighting

**Example Prompt Elements:** "space opera scene, massive space station, diverse alien architecture, starships, cosmic background, nebula, advanced technology, epic scale, science fiction"

## Post-Apocalyptic

**Core Concept:** After civilization collapse, survival focus, reclaimed ruins, scarcity

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Ruined buildings
- Overgrown nature reclaiming cities
- Makeshift repairs
- Scavenged technology
- Dust and decay
- Desperate fortifications

**Color Palette:** Rust browns, dusty greys, faded colors, ash, dried blood, nature greens reclaiming

**Atmosphere:** Desolate, desperate survival, remnants of lost world, dangerous

**Common Elements:**
- Crumbling skyscrapers
- Makeshift shelters in ruins
- Scrap metal walls
- Radiation zones
- Overgrown highways
- Survivor camps
- Raider outposts
- Nature overtaking civilization

**Map Features:**
- Dead cities
- Survivor settlements in ruins
- Dangerous zones (radiation, mutants)
- Resource locations (water, supplies)
- Trade routes between settlements
- No-man's land
- Nature reclamation areas

**Lighting:** Harsh sunlight through haze, fires in barrels, dim salvaged lights, toxic glows

**Example Prompt Elements:** "post-apocalyptic landscape, ruined city, overgrown buildings, rust and decay, survivor settlement, makeshift structures, desolate atmosphere, nature reclaiming civilization"

## Horror

**Core Concept:** Fear and dread, cosmic horror, psychological terror, the unknown

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Disturbing proportions
- Fog and darkness
- Things half-seen
- Unnatural angles
- Decay and corruption
- Isolation

**Color Palette:** Blacks, greys, sickly greens, blood reds, unnatural purples, pale flesh tones

**Atmosphere:** Dread, fear, isolation, wrongness, sanity-threatening

**Common Elements:**
- Abandoned locations
- Fog-shrouded areas
- Twisted architecture
- Signs of violence
- Unnatural phenomena
- Isolation
- Haunted structures
- Eldritch symbols

**Map Features:**
- Isolated locations
- Haunted areas
- Forbidden zones
- Reality distortions
- Underground horrors
- Cursed sites
- Asylum/hospital ruins

**Lighting:** Deep shadows, flickering lights, unnatural glows, almost total darkness, pale moonlight

**Example Prompt Elements:** "horror landscape, fog-shrouded ruins, disturbing atmosphere, deep shadows, abandoned building, eerie lighting, psychological horror, sense of dread, darkness"

## Eastern/Wuxia

**Core Concept:** Martial arts fantasy, spiritual cultivation, honor and duty, Asian mythology

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Curved roofs with upturned eaves
- Martial artists in flowing robes
- Mountain peaks shrouded in mist
- Bamboo forests
- Ancient temples
- Mystical martial arts effects
- Calligraphy and scrolls

**Color Palette:** Red and gold (imperial), jade green, mountain mists (grey/white), natural wood, silk colors

**Atmosphere:** Martial honor, spiritual journey, harmonious but dangerous, legendary

**Common Elements:**
- Mountaintop temples
- Martial arts schools
- Bamboo forests
- Sacred mountains
- Imperial palaces
- Hidden valleys
- Flowing robes and weapons
- Mystical effects from chi/ki

**Map Features:**
- Sacred mountains
- Hidden martial schools
- Rivers and waterfalls
- Ancient forests
- Imperial cities
- Mystical locations
- Hermit caves

**Lighting:** Morning mist, golden hour, moonlight on water, mystical chi glows, lanterns

**Example Prompt Elements:** "wuxia landscape, misty mountains, ancient Chinese temple, martial artists, bamboo forest, mystical atmosphere, flowing robes, traditional architecture, spiritual energy effects"

## Mythological

**Core Concept:** Based on real-world mythologies, gods and heroes, legendary creatures

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Architecture from specific mythology (Greek, Norse, Egyptian, etc.)
- Divine/supernatural elements
- Legendary creatures
- Heroic scale
- Sacred sites
- Mythic symbolism

**Color Palette:** Depends on mythology - Greek (marble white, gold, blue), Norse (grey, ice blue, brown), Egyptian (gold, blue, sand)

**Atmosphere:** Legendary, timeless, intervention of gods, fate and prophecy

**Common Elements:**
- Greek: Mount Olympus, temples, amphitheaters, Mediterranean
- Norse: Halls like Valhalla, world tree, ice and snow, longships
- Egyptian: Pyramids, temples, desert, the Nile, hieroglyphs
- Divine manifestations
- Legendary beasts
- Heroes and quests

**Map Features:**
- Sacred sites
- Homes of gods
- Legendary locations (Labyrinth, Asgard, etc.)
- Mythical geography (River Styx, Bifrost)
- Monster lairs
- Oracle locations

**Lighting:** Divine radiance, sacred light, appropriate to mythology

**Example Prompt Elements:** "Greek mythology landscape, ancient temple, Mediterranean setting, divine atmosphere, marble architecture, Mount Olympus, legendary heroes, gods' presence, epic scale"

## Weird West

**Core Concept:** American Old West + supernatural/sci-fi elements, frontier horror

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Western architecture (saloons, frontier towns)
- Desert landscapes
- Addition of supernatural/alien elements
- Steam-tech or magic mixed with revolvers
- Gothic horror in Western setting

**Color Palette:** Desert tans/browns, dusty reds, sun-bleached wood, addition of supernatural colors (unnatural greens, eerie purples)

**Atmosphere:** Frontier isolation, supernatural dread, lawless with occult elements

**Common Elements:**
- Saloons with arcane symbols
- Ghost towns (literally)
- Native American mysticism
- Steam-powered wagons
- Undead gunslingers
- Desert with strange phenomena
- Mining towns with eldritch discoveries
- Railroad connecting weird locations

**Map Features:**
- Frontier towns
- Haunted deserts
- Cursed gold mines
- Sacred native sites
- Unexplained phenomena locations
- Railroad routes
- Outlaw hideouts with twist

**Lighting:** Harsh desert sun, dramatic dusk, eerie night, supernatural glows in darkness

**Example Prompt Elements:** "weird west landscape, frontier town, supernatural elements, desert setting, Gothic horror, Old West architecture, occult symbols, steam technology, eerie atmosphere"

## Solarpunk

**Core Concept:** Optimistic future, sustainability, harmony with nature, renewable energy, hope

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Green architecture (living walls, rooftop gardens)
- Art Nouveau influences
- Solar panels integrated beautifully
- Wind turbines as art
- Nature integrated with technology
- Bright, clean aesthetics

**Color Palette:** Greens, solar panel blues, natural browns, bright sky blues, flower colors, clean whites

**Atmosphere:** Hopeful, sustainable, harmonious, community-focused, bright future

**Common Elements:**
- Vertical gardens
- Solar panel arrays (beautiful)
- Wind farms
- Green public spaces
- Community centers
- Bicycle/pedestrian paths
- Water reclamation
- Beautiful sustainable architecture

**Map Features:**
- Green cities
- Interconnected communities
- Public transit networks
- Community farms
- Renewable energy sites
- Restored natural areas
- Walkable neighborhoods

**Lighting:** Natural sunlight emphasized, solar reflections, bioluminescent plants (night), golden hour

**Example Prompt Elements:** "solarpunk city, sustainable architecture, vertical gardens, solar panels, Art Nouveau design, harmonious with nature, bright optimistic atmosphere, green technology, community spaces"

## Grimdark

**Core Concept:** Unrelenting darkness, no heroes only survivors, brutal, nihilistic

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Extreme decay
- Brutal aesthetics
- War-torn everything
- Skulls and death imagery
- Industrial nightmare
- Corruption visible everywhere

**Color Palette:** Blacks, dark greys, rust reds, toxic yellows, sickly greens, dried blood browns

**Atmosphere:** Oppressive, hopeless, brutal, survival at any cost, constant war/conflict

**Common Elements:**
- Hive cities (massive dystopian)
- Perpetual war zones
- Industrial nightmares
- Corrupted wastelands
- Fortresses always under siege
- Death and skulls as decoration
- Religious fanaticism
- Technology as brutal tools

**Map Features:**
- War-torn regions
- Hive cities (vertical slums)
- No-man's lands
- Fortified positions
- Corrupted zones
- Industrial hellscapes
- Constant conflict borders

**Lighting:** Harsh industrial, toxic glows, fires of war, oppressive darkness, searchlights

**Example Prompt Elements:** "grimdark landscape, dystopian war-torn city, brutal architecture, skulls and death imagery, industrial nightmare, oppressive atmosphere, rust and decay, no hope, constant warfare"
