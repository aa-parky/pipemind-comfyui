# Regional Map Examples

Complete examples of regional/local area maps with descriptions and optimized prompts for z-image turbo.

## Example 1: Fantasy Village and Surroundings

### Description
The village of Millbrook and its surrounding area (10-mile radius). Fortified village near a river with a mill. Farmland surrounds the village. Dark forest to the north (monster habitat). Ancient ruins on eastern hills. Goblin cave in the forest.

### Optimized Prompt
```
Fantasy regional map, top-down view, showing fortified village Millbrook in center with wooden palisade walls, watermill on river flowing west to east through southern portion, surrounding farmland with field patterns, dirt roads connecting village to outlying farms, dark forest in northern third marked with dense tree symbols, ancient stone ruins on eastern hills, goblin cave entrance marked in northwest forest, small shrine at crossroads south of village, river bridge connecting north and south roads, hand-drawn on parchment style, warm earth tones with dark green forest, clear readable labels, adventure game map aesthetic, detailed but functional, high quality
```

## Example 2: Cyberpunk City District

### Description
The Neon District, a cyberpunk entertainment and black market zone. Main street (Synapse Avenue) runs through center. Corporate tower in southwest corner. Underground entrance to hacker haven. Numerous clubs, shops, and back alleys.

### Optimized Prompt
```
Cyberpunk district map, top-down view, showing 6-block urban area, main street Synapse Avenue running vertically through center with neon-lit establishments, narrow alleyways between buildings, corporate megabuilding in southwest corner, underground metro entrance marked with stairs symbol in center, hacker den entrance hidden in northeast alley, nightclub district in western blocks, black market in eastern section, elevated highway crossing diagonally in northwest, building outlines clear, streets and alleys well-defined, neon color highlights on pink and blue against dark grey background, tactical map style, grid-like city layout, labels for key locations, high contrast, modern urban aesthetic, high quality
```

## Example 3: Wilderness Hex Crawl

### Description
Untamed wilderness region for exploration. Mixed terrain: forests, hills, swamps, and ruins. Hexagonal grid for travel. Several points of interest to discover. River system running through region.

### Optimized Prompt
```
Wilderness exploration map, top-down view, hexagonal grid overlay, showing wild frontier region approximately 60 miles across, mixed terrain with temperate forests in northeast, rolling hills in west, swampland in southern section, river flowing from northwest to southeast, abandoned watchtower ruins in center, hidden bandit camp in southwest forest, ancient shrine in hills, cave system in northern hills marked, overgrown road remnants visible, small stream tributaries, hex grid clearly visible but not overwhelming terrain, illustrated adventure map style, natural color palette with greens, browns, and blues, sense of unexplored territory, clear hexes for gameplay, high detail, game-ready quality
```

## Example 4: Space Station District Map

### Description
Mid-section residential and commercial district of orbital station Aurora. Main promenade, residential blocks, commercial area, docking ports, maintenance access tunnels.

### Optimized Prompt
```
Science fiction space station district map, top-down view, showing circular section of station, central promenade as main circular corridor, residential modules as rectangular blocks radiating outward, commercial district along inner ring with shops and restaurants, four docking ports at cardinal directions, maintenance access tunnels shown as narrow passages between modules, emergency airlock positions marked, hydroponic garden in eastern section, security station near docking port, elevators to other decks marked, metallic floor texture, walls clearly defined, clean sci-fi aesthetic, technical schematic style, cool blue and grey color scheme, labeled areas, space station architecture, high quality technical map
```

## Example 5: Haunted Castle Grounds

### Description
Castle Ravencrest and its surrounding grounds. The castle sits on a hill overlooking a dead forest. Graveyard nearby. Abandoned village at the base of the hill. Mist-shrouded atmosphere.

### Optimized Prompt
```
Gothic horror regional map, top-down view, showing Castle Ravencrest on central hill, large Gothic castle with multiple towers, winding path up hill from abandoned village at base, dead forest surrounding area with bare twisted trees, graveyard west of castle with visible tombstones, ruins of old church in village, stone bridge crossing dried ravine, perpetual mist suggested by atmospheric fading at edges, dead and dying vegetation, ominous atmosphere, dark color palette with greys, blacks, and muted purples, Gothic aesthetic, horror game map style, detailed and atmospheric, high quality
```
